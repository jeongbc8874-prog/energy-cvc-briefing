■ Layer 2 — The Transformer Bottleneck: Why It Matters
■ Why Transformers Are Bottleneck
EHV (Extra High Voltage) transformers are custom-engineered per project. No two are identical. Manufacturing is dominated by 3 global players — ABB, Siemens, Hitachi — all operating at capacity.

Lead time: 36 months. That means a hyperscaler signing a data center lease today won't have power until 2028 at earliest.

The interconnection queue lists 2,290GW of projects waiting. Most will never get power — not because of permitting, but because there are no transformers.
IRR IMPLICATION: Every 6-month COD delay = ~150bps IRR reduction on $500M+ data center project
